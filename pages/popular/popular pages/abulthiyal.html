Recipe.lk - Malu Abul Thiyal (Sri Lankan Fish Sour Curry)

Ready In: 45 mins

Ingredients: 12

Serves: 4 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Fish (Tuna or any firm fish) - 500g, cut into pieces
  • Goraka - 2-3 pieces (soaked in water)
  • Ginger - 1 inch, minced
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Green chilies - 2, sliced
  • Shallots - 3, sliced
  • Black pepper - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cinnamon stick - 1 small piece
  • Cloves - 2-3
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Water - as needed

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Soak the Goraka in water for about 10-15 minutes to soften.
  • In a pot, add the fish pieces, curry leaves, garlic, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, turmeric powder, and black pepper.
  • Crush the Goraka and add it to the pot along with water (enough to cover the fish). Add salt to taste.
  • Bring it to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, allowing the fish to cook through.
  • Once the fish is tender, add the shallots and green chilies and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  • If needed, adjust the seasoning and sourness with more Goraka or salt.
  • Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes to let the flavors develop.
  • Serve the Malu Abul Thiyal hot with rice or roti.
