Recipe.lk - Sri Lankan Cutlet Recipe

Ready In: 1 hour

Ingredients: 15

Serves: 6 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Minced Meat (Chicken, Beef, or Fish) - 300g
  • Boiled Potatoes - 2 large
  • Onion - 1, finely chopped
  • Green Chilies - 2, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1-inch piece, minced
  • Carrot - 1 small, grated
  • Curry Leaves - a few, chopped
  • Coriander Powder - 1 tsp
  • Cumin Powder - 1 tsp
  • Chili Powder - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black Pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Breadcrumbs - for coating
  • Egg - 1, beaten (for coating)
  • Oil - for frying

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • In a large pan, cook the minced meat over medium heat, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  • Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies to the pan. Saut√© until onions turn golden brown.
  • Add grated carrots, curry leaves, coriander powder, cumin powder, chili powder, black pepper, and salt. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  • Once cooked, remove the mixture from the heat and let it cool down to room temperature.
  • In a separate bowl, mash the boiled potatoes and combine them with the cooled meat mixture. Mix everything thoroughly until well combined.
  • Shape the mixture into small oval or round cutlets.
  • Dip each cutlet in beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  • Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Fry the cutlets until golden brown and crispy on both sides (about 4-5 minutes per side).
  • Remove the cutlets from the oil and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  • Serve the cutlets hot with a dipping sauce or salad on the side.
