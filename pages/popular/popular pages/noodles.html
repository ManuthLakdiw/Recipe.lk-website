Recipe.lk - Noodles Recipe

Ready In: 20 mins

Ingredients: 12

Serves: 3-4 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Noodles - 200g (egg noodles or instant noodles)
  • Carrots - 1/2 cup, thinly sliced
  • Green Beans - 1/2 cup, thinly sliced
  • Cabbage - 1/2 cup, shredded
  • Onion - 1 medium, thinly sliced
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Green Chilies - 2, slit (optional)
  • Eggs - 2, beaten (optional)
  • Soy Sauce - 2 tbsp
  • Chili Sauce - 1 tbsp (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Pepper - to taste
  • Oil - 3 tbsp

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  • Add beaten eggs (if using) and scramble until cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add the remaining oil. Saut√© sliced onions, garlic, and green chilies until fragrant.
  • Add sliced carrots, green beans, and shredded cabbage. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  • Add the cooked noodles to the pan. Break up any clumps and mix well with the vegetables.
  • Add soy sauce, chili sauce (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly to ensure the noodles are evenly coated.
  • Add the scrambled eggs back to the pan and mix well.
  • Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until everything is heated through.
  • Serve hot with a side of chili sauce or ketchup.
