Recipe.lk - Koththu Roti Recipe

Ready In: 30 mins

Ingredients: 15

Serves: 3-4 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Roti (Godamba Roti or Paratha) - 4, shredded into small pieces
  • Chicken, Beef, or Vegetables - 200g (cooked and shredded)
  • Onion - 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 4 cloves, minced
  • Green Chilies - 2, finely chopped
  • Carrot - 1/2 cup, finely chopped
  • Leek - 1/2 cup, finely chopped
  • Cabbage - 1/2 cup, shredded
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Curry Leaves - 1 sprig
  • Eggs - 2 (optional)
  • Soy Sauce - 1 tbsp
  • Chili Powder - 1 tsp (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 3 tbsp
  • Spring Onions - for garnish

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large pan or flat griddle over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions, garlic, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until the onions are soft and golden.
  • Add chopped carrots, leeks, and cabbage. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  • Push the vegetables to one side of the pan. Add the remaining oil and crack the eggs (if using). Scramble the eggs until cooked.
  • Add the shredded roti pieces to the pan. Mix well with the vegetables and eggs.
  • Add the cooked chicken, beef, or vegetables. Mix thoroughly.
  • Add soy sauce, chili powder (if using), and salt. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes, ensuring everything is well combined.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Garnish with chopped spring onions.
  • Serve hot with a side of spicy curry sauce or chili sauce.
