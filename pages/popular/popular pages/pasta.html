Recipe.lk - Pasta Recipe

Ready In: 25 mins

Ingredients: 12

Serves: 3-4 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Pasta - 200g (penne, spaghetti, or your choice)
  • Onion - 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Carrot - 1/2 cup, grated
  • Green Chilies - 2, slit (optional)
  • Chicken or Vegetables - 200g (optional, cooked and shredded)
  • Tomato Sauce - 1/2 cup
  • Chili Powder - 1 tsp (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Pepper - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Cheese - for garnish (optional)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions, garlic, and green chilies. Saut√© until the onions are soft and golden.
  • Add grated carrots and chopped tomatoes. Cook for 3-4 minutes until the tomatoes soften.
  • Add cooked chicken or vegetables (if using) and mix well.
  • Add tomato sauce, chili powder (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add the cooked pasta to the pan. Mix thoroughly to coat the pasta with the sauce.
  • Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until everything is heated through.
  • Garnish with grated cheese (if using) and serve hot.
