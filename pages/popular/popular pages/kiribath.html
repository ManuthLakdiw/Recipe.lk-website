Recipe.lk - Kiribath (Sri Lankan Milk Rice)

Ready In: 30 mins

Ingredients: 5

Serves: 4 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Raw Rice - 1 1/2 cups (preferably short-grain rice)
  • Coconut Milk - 1 1/2 cups
  • Water - 1 cup
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon (or to taste)
  • Freshly grated coconut - 1/4 cup (optional, for garnish)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Rinse the rice well and drain it.
  • In a large pot, combine the rice, coconut milk, and water.
  • Add salt and bring it to a boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  • Reduce the heat and cover the pot. Let the rice cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the liquid is absorbed and the rice is tender.
  • If you want a creamier texture, you can stir in a tablespoon of ghee at this stage.
  • Once cooked, remove from heat and let it sit for a few minutes. The rice should be soft and sticky.
  • Transfer the Kiribath to a serving dish and flatten it with a spoon or your hands to shape it.
  • Optionally, garnish with freshly grated coconut for extra flavor and texture.
  • Serve the Kiribath with Lunu Miris (spicy sambol) depending on your preference.
