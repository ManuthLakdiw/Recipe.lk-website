Recipe.lk - Sri Lankan Roti Recipe

Ready In: 20 mins

Ingredients: 5

Serves: 4 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Flour - 2 cups
  • Water - 1 cup
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Butter (optional) - for brushing

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • In a mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  • Gradually add water while mixing the flour to form a dough.
  • Knead the dough for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  • Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 10-15 minutes.
  • Divide the dough into small balls and roll each one into a flat circle.
  • Heat a tawa or frying pan on medium heat and cook each roti for 1-2 minutes on each side, until golden brown spots appear.
  • Brush with butter, if desired, and serve hot with curry or sambol.
