Recipe.lk - Parippu Vade (Lentil Fritters) Recipe

Ready In: 45 minutes

Ingredients: 12

Serves: 4-6 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Lentils (Yellow or Red) - 1 cup
  • Onion - 1, finely chopped
  • Green Chilies - 2, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1-inch piece, minced
  • Curry Leaves - a few, chopped
  • Fennel Seeds - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Coriander Powder - 1 tsp
  • Cumin Powder - 1 tsp
  • Chili Powder - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Water - as needed
  • Oil - for frying

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Soak the lentils in water for about 2 hours. Drain the lentils well after soaking.
  • Grind the soaked lentils in a food processor or using a mortar and pestle, leaving some texture. Don't make it into a smooth paste.
  • In a mixing bowl, combine the ground lentils, chopped onion, green chilies, garlic, ginger, curry leaves, fennel seeds, coriander powder, cumin powder, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add a little water, just enough to bind the mixture together into a thick dough-like consistency.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Take a small amount of the mixture and shape it into a flat round disc (about 1/2-inch thick) using your palms or fingers.
  • Carefully drop the shaped dough into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy on both sides (around 4-5 minutes per side).
  • Remove the fritters from the oil and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  • Serve hot with coconut chutney, sambol, or a cup of tea.
