Recipe.lk - Pittu Recipe

Ready In: 30 mins

Ingredients: 6

Serves: 3-4 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice Flour - 2 cups
  • Grated Coconut - 1 cup (fresh or desiccated)
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp (or to taste)
  • Warm Water - 1/2 cup (or as needed)
  • Coconut Milk - 1/2 cup (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Water - for steaming

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine rice flour and salt. Mix well.
  • Gradually add warm water (and coconut milk if using) to the rice flour. Mix with your hands until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  • Add grated coconut to the mixture and combine well.
  • Prepare a steamer or pittu maker. Fill the bottom part with water and bring it to a boil.
  • Layer the pittu mixture and grated coconut alternately in the pittu maker. Start with a layer of grated coconut, then add the rice flour mixture, and repeat until the pittu maker is filled.
  • Place the pittu maker on the steamer and steam for 10-15 minutes, or until the pittu is cooked through.
  • Once cooked, remove the pittu maker from the steamer and let it cool slightly.
  • Push the pittu out of the maker using a wooden stick or spoon.
  • Serve hot with coconut milk, curry, or sambol.
