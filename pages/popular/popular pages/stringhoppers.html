Recipe.lk - String Hoppers (Idiyappam) Recipe

Ready In: 45 mins

Ingredients: 5

Serves: 3-4 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice Flour - 2 cups (fine variety)
  • Water - 1 1/2 cups (hot)
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp (or to taste)
  • Coconut Milk - 1/2 cup (optional, for flavor)
  • Oil - 1 tsp (for greasing)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine rice flour and salt. Mix well.
  • Gradually add hot water (and coconut milk if using) to the rice flour. Mix with a spoon until it forms a dough-like consistency.
  • Knead the dough well until it becomes smooth and pliable. Cover and let it rest for 10-15 minutes.
  • Prepare a string hopper maker (idiyappam press) by greasing the inside lightly with oil.
  • Take a small portion of the dough and place it in the string hopper maker. Press the dough onto a greased string hopper mold or banana leaf to form circular nests.
  • Steam the string hoppers in a steamer for 5-7 minutes, or until they are cooked through and firm.
  • Remove the string hoppers from the steamer and let them cool slightly.
  • Serve warm with coconut sambol, curry, or coconut milk.
