Recipe.lk - Biriyani Recipe

Ready In: 1 hour 30 mins

Ingredients: 20

Serves: 4-6 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Basmati Rice - 2 cups
  • Chicken, Beef, or Mutton - 500g, cut into pieces
  • Onion - 2 large, thinly sliced
  • Tomato - 2, chopped
  • Garlic - 6 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1-inch piece, grated
  • Green Chilies - 4, slit
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Biriyani Masala - 2 tbsp
  • Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
  • Chili Powder - 1 tsp (optional)
  • Cinnamon Stick - 1 small piece
  • Cardamom Pods - 4
  • Cloves - 4
  • Bay Leaves - 2
  • Mint Leaves - 1/2 cup, chopped
  • Coriander Leaves - 1/2 cup, chopped
  • Ghee or Oil - 4 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Saffron - a pinch (soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk, optional)
  • Fried Onions - for garnish (optional)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Wash and soak the basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat 2 tablespoons of ghee or oil in a large pot. Add cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and bay leaves. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.
  • Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add the chicken, beef, or mutton pieces. Cook until the meat is browned.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, chili powder (if using), and biriyani masala. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Add yogurt, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Stir well and cook for another 5 minutes.
  • In a separate pot, boil 4 cups of water. Add the soaked and drained rice. Cook until the rice is 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  • Layer the partially cooked rice over the meat mixture in the pot. Drizzle the saffron milk (if using) and remaining ghee or oil on top.
  • Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes (dum cooking).
  • Once done, gently fluff the biriyani with a fork. Garnish with fried onions (if using).
  • Serve hot with raita or a side salad.
