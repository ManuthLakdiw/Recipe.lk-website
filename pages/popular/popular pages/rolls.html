Recipe.lk - Sri Lankan Rolls Recipe

Ready In: 45 mins

Ingredients: 12

Serves: 10-12 rolls

INGREDIENTS

  • Potatoes - 3 medium, boiled and mashed
  • Chicken or Fish - 200g, cooked and shredded (optional)
  • Onion - 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Green Chilies - 2, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Curry Leaves - 1 sprig, chopped
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Chili Powder - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Eggs - 2, beaten
  • Breadcrumbs - 1 cup
  • Oil - for frying
  • Spring Roll Wrappers - 10-12

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan. Add chopped onions, garlic, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until the onions are soft and golden.
  • Add turmeric powder, chili powder (if using), and salt. Mix well.
  • Add the mashed potatoes and shredded chicken or fish (if using). Mix thoroughly and cook for 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  • Take a spring roll wrapper and place 2 tablespoons of the potato mixture in the center. Roll tightly, folding the sides inward to seal.
  • Dip the rolled wrapper in beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Fry the rolls until golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  • Serve hot with ketchup or chili sauce.
