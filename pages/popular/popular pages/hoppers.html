Recipe.lk - Sri Lankan Appa (Hoppers)

Ready In: 2 hours

Ingredients: 8

Serves: 4-6 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice Flour - 2 cups
  • Coconut Milk - 1 cup
  • Warm Water - 1/2 cup
  • Yeast - 1 teaspoon
  • Sugar - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Oil - for greasing
  • Warm Water (for fermenting) - 1/4 cup

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • In a bowl, combine rice flour, salt, sugar, and yeast. Add warm water and mix well.
  • Slowly add coconut milk to the mixture while stirring until you form a smooth batter.
  • Cover the bowl with a cloth and let the batter rest in a warm place for 1-2 hours for fermentation.
  • After fermentation, the batter should be bubbly and slightly risen. Stir the batter gently.
  • Heat a small, round hopper pan (appa pan) and lightly grease it with oil.
  • Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the pan and swirl it to coat the sides of the pan.
  • Cover the pan and cook on low heat for about 3-4 minutes until the edges become crispy and golden brown.
  • Remove the appa carefully and repeat the process until all the batter is used up.
  • Serve hot with Lunu Miris (spicy sambol) or curry.
