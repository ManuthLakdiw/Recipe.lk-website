Nature's Elixirs: The Healing Power of Herbal Drinks

Herbal drinks have long been cherished for their natural healing properties and ability to rejuvenate both body and mind. Made from various plant-based ingredients such as leaves, flowers, roots, and herbs, these beverages offer a nourishing blend of flavors that are both soothing and therapeutic.

RECIPE

Kolakenda (Herbal Green Porridge)

Kolakenda – A traditional herbal drink that has been a staple in Sri Lankan households for generations, Kolakenda is a nourishing and rejuvenating green porridge made from a blend of leafy herbs and other local herbs.

Koththamalli (Coriander Drink)

Koththamalli – A refreshing herbal drink made from fresh coriander leaves, Koththamalli is not only a flavorful beverage but also a powerhouse of health benefits.This drink is often consumed to improve digestion

RECIPE

Ranawara

Ranawara – A soothing herbal tea made from the dried flowers of the Cassia Auriculata plant, Ranawara is widely known for its cooling and medicinal properties. This caffeine-free beverage helps to regulate body heat, purify blood, and promote relaxation.
