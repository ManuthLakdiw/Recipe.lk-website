Recipe.lk - Watermelon Juice Recipe

Ready In: 5 minutes

Ingredients: 4

Serves: 2 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups Watermelon, cubed (seeds removed)
  • 1-2 tbsp Sugar or Honey (optional)
  • 1/2 cup Cold Water or Coconut Water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Cut the watermelon into cubes, removing any seeds.
  • Place the watermelon cubes in a blender.
  • Add sugar or honey, and cold water (or coconut water) to the blender.
  • Blend until smooth. If you prefer, strain the juice to remove pulp.
  • Serve in a glass over ice cubes, if desired, and enjoy the refreshing watermelon juice.
