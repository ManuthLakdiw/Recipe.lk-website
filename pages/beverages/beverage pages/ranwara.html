Recipe.lk - Ranawara Tea Recipe

Ready In: 15 minutes

Ingredients: 3

Serves: 2 cups

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tbsp Dried Ranawara Flowers
  • 2 cups Water
  • Honey or Jaggery (to taste, optional)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Boil 2 cups of water in a pot.
  • Add dried Ranawara flowers to the boiling water.
  • Simmer for 10 minutes to extract the flavors.
  • Strain the tea into a cup.
  • Add honey or jaggery if desired, and serve warm.
