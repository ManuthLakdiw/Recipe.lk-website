Recipe.lk - Avocado Juice Recipe

Ready In: 10 minutes

Ingredients: 5

Serves: 2 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 ripe Avocado
  • 1 cup Cold Milk
  • 1-2 tbsp Sugar (or honey)
  • Ice cubes - a handful
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract (optional)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Cut the avocado in half and remove the pit. Scoop out the flesh into a blender.
  • Add cold milk, sugar (or honey), and vanilla extract (optional) to the blender.
  • Add ice cubes to make the juice cold and refreshing.
  • Blend everything until smooth and creamy.
  • Pour the avocado juice into glasses and serve chilled.
