Recipe.lk - Kolakanda (Gotukola Herbal Porridge) Recipe

Ready In: 20 minutes

Ingredients: 6

Serves: 2-3 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup Gotukola (Centella asiatica) leaves, washed
  • 1/2 cup Cooked Rice (or raw rice soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 cup Thick Coconut Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Salt (or to taste)
  • 1 tbsp Jaggery or Sugar (optional, for sweetness)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Blend Gotukola leaves with 1 cup of water until smooth. Strain and set aside.
  • In a pot, add cooked rice with 1 cup of water and bring to a boil.
  • Once the rice softens, pour in the Gotukola juice and mix well.
  • Add salt and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Pour in the coconut milk, stir well, and cook for another 2 minutes on low heat.
  • Turn off the heat, add jaggery or sugar if desired, and serve warm.
