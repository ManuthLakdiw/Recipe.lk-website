Recipe.lk - Pomegranate Juice Recipe

Ready In: 10 minutes

Ingredients: 4

Serves: 2 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 large Pomegranate
  • 1-2 tbsp Sugar (or honey, optional)
  • 1/2 cup Cold Water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Cut the pomegranate in half and remove the seeds (also called arils).
  • Place the pomegranate seeds in a blender.
  • Add sugar (or honey) and cold water to the blender.
  • Blend until smooth. If you prefer, strain the juice to remove the pulp.
  • Serve in a glass over ice cubes, if desired, and enjoy the refreshing pomegranate juice.
