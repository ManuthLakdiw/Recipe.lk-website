Recipe.lk - Koththamalli (Coriander Tea) Recipe

Ready In: 10 minutes

Ingredients: 5

Serves: 2 cups

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tbsp Coriander Seeds
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 small piece Ginger (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper (optional)
  • Honey or Jaggery (to taste, optional)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Crush the coriander seeds slightly to release the flavor.
  • In a pot, add water and bring to a boil.
  • Add the crushed coriander seeds, ginger, and black pepper.
  • Simmer for 5-7 minutes until the water reduces slightly.
  • Strain the tea into a cup.
  • Add honey or jaggery if desired, and serve warm.
