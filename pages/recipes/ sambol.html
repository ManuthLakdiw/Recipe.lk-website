Spice Infusion: Zesty Sambols & Fiery Flavors

Step into a world of heat and flavor where every bite ignites your taste buds! This category is a true haven for spice enthusiasts, featuring an irresistible collection of fiery condiments Get ready to spice up your plate and master the art of Sri Lankan heat!

RECIPE

Pol Sambol

Pol Sambol – A fiery and tangy coconut relish that brings a vibrant kick to any meal. A staple in Sri Lankan cuisine, it pairs perfectly with rice, hoppers, or bread, adding depth and enhancing digestion. Bursting with authentic flavors, this simple yet bold condiment elevates every bite.

RECIPE

Spicy Sambol

Spicy Sambol (Lunu Miris) – A fiery, tangy condiment that packs a punch in every bite. This traditional Sri Lankan sambol, also known as Lunu Miris, is a popular accompaniment to rice, hoppers, and roti, adding a burst of heat and flavor to your meals.

Chili Paste

Chili Paste – A fiery and flavorful Sri Lankan condiment made from fresh, ground red chilies. The paste is crafted by finely grinding chilies with other spices, creating a thick making it the perfect addition to your kitchen for a variety of dishes that crave a spicy kick.
