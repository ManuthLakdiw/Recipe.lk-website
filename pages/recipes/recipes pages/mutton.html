Recipe.lk - Mutton Curry Recipe

Ready In: 1 hour 30 mins

Ingredients: 15

Serves: 4-5 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Mutton - 500g, cut into pieces
  • Onion - 2 medium, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 4 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1-inch piece, grated
  • Green Chilies - 2, slit
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Curry Leaves - 2 sprigs
  • Cinnamon Stick - 1 small piece
  • Cardamom Pods - 3
  • Cloves - 4
  • Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
  • Chili Powder - 1 tbsp (adjust to taste)
  • Curry Powder - 1 tbsp
  • Coconut Milk - 1 cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 3 tbsp

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Clean and wash the mutton pieces thoroughly. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat 3 tablespoons of oil in a large pot. Add cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.
  • Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until the onions are golden brown.
  • Add curry leaves and sauté for another minute.
  • Add the mutton pieces to the pot and stir well to coat them with the onion-spice mixture.
  • Add turmeric powder, chili powder, and curry powder. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Add the chopped tomato and cook until it softens and forms a thick paste.
  • Add 2 cups of water and salt to taste. Stir well, cover, and simmer on low heat for 1 hour or until the mutton is tender.
  • Once the mutton is tender, add 1 cup of coconut milk and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  • Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed. Taste and adjust seasoning if required.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot with rice, roti, or bread.
