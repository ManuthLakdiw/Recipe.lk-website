Recipe.lk - Dhal Curry Recipe

Ready In: 15 mins

Ingredients: 14

Serves: 3-4 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Red Lentils (Dhal) - 1 cup
  • Onion - 1, sliced
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, crushed
  • Green Chilies - 1-2, slit
  • Ginger - 1 small piece, grated
  • Tomatoes - 2, chopped
  • Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
  • Cumin Seeds - 1 tsp
  • Mustard Seeds - 1 tsp
  • Curry Leaves - 1 sprig
  • Cinnamon Stick - 1 small piece (optional)
  • Chili Powder - 1 tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Coconut Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Salt - to taste

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Rinse 1 cup of lentils thoroughly in cold water and set aside.
  • In a pot, add the lentils and 3 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then simmer until the lentils are soft (15-20 minutes).
  • In a separate pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  • Add sliced onion, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© until soft and fragrant.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, and chili powder. Cook for 5 minutes until tomatoes soften.
  • Add the onion-tomato mixture to the cooked lentils and stir well.
  • Add 1/2 cup of coconut milk, stir, and let it simmer for another 5-10 minutes on low heat.
  • If the curry is too thick, add water to adjust the consistency.
  • Remove from heat and serve with rice or roti.
