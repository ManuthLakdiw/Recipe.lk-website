Recipe.lk - Jackfruit Curry Recipe

Ready In: 45 mins

Ingredients: 10

Serves: 4 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Raw Jackfruit - 500g (peeled and cut into pieces)
  • Onion - 1, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1 inch, minced
  • Curry Leaves - a few
  • Green Chilies - 2-3 (slit lengthwise)
  • Coconut Milk - 1 cup
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Curry Powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until soft and fragrant.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, curry leaves, and green chilies. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  • Add curry powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Stir to combine the spices.
  • Add the raw jackfruit pieces and mix well. Sauté for 5 minutes to coat the jackfruit with the spices.
  • Pour in the coconut milk and add water if needed to cover the jackfruit.
  • Cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the jackfruit is tender and cooked through.
  • Adjust salt and seasoning to taste.
  • Serve the Jackfruit Curry hot with rice or roti.
