Recipe.lk - Sri Lankan Pol Sambol

Ready In: 15 mins

Ingredients: 6

Serves: 4 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Fresh Coconut - 1 cup (grated)
  • Red Onion - 1 small, finely chopped
  • Green Chilies - 2-3, chopped
  • Chili Powder - 1-2 teaspoons (adjust to taste)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Lime or Lemon Juice - 1 tablespoon
  • Fried Maldive Fish (optional) - 1 tablespoon

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Grate fresh coconut or use pre-grated coconut.
  • In a bowl, add the grated coconut, chopped red onion, and green chilies.
  • Add chili powder, salt, and lime/lemon juice to the mixture.
  • If you prefer, you can add fried Maldive fish for extra flavor.
  • Mix all the ingredients thoroughly to combine them well.
  • Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  • Serve the Pol Sambol as a side dish with rice or roti.
