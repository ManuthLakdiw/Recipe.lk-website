Recipe.lk - Spicy Sri Lankan Fish Curry

Ready In: 20 mins

Ingredients: 13

Serves: 4 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Fish (like Mackerel or Tilapia) - 500g, cut into pieces
  • Onion - 1, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1 inch, minced
  • Curry Leaves - a few
  • Green Chilies - 3-4, slit lengthwise
  • Chili Powder - 1 to 2 teaspoons (for heat)
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Curry Powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Goraka (dried) - 1 to 2 pieces (soaked in warm water)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until soft and fragrant.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, curry leaves, and green chilies. Cook until the tomatoes soften.
  • Add chili powder, turmeric powder, and curry powder. Stir well to combine.
  • Add the fish pieces, and mix gently to coat the fish in the spices.
  • Add soaked goraka pieces (remove seeds if needed), then pour in enough water to cover the fish.
  • Let the curry simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the fish is cooked and the flavors meld.
  • Adjust salt to taste and remove from heat.
  • Serve the Spicy Sri Lankan Fish Curry with rice or roti.
