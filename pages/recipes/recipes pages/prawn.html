Recipe.lk - Sri Lankan Prawn Devilled

Ready In: 30 mins

Ingredients: 10

Serves: 2-4 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Prawns - 500g (peeled and deveined)
  • Onion - 1, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Green Chilies - 2-3, slit lengthwise
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1-inch piece, minced
  • Chili Sauce - 2 tablespoons
  • Soya Sauce - 1 tablespoon
  • Vinegar - 1 tablespoon
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - to taste
  • Sugar - 1 teaspoon (optional)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  • Add green chilies and chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  • Add chili sauce, soya sauce, vinegar, and sugar (optional). Stir to combine.
  • Add the prawns and cook for 5-7 minutes, ensuring the prawns are coated in the sauce.
  • Adjust salt and seasoning to taste. If needed, add a little water to create more sauce.
  • Once the prawns are cooked, remove from heat.
  • Serve the Prawn Devilled hot with rice or as an appetizer.
