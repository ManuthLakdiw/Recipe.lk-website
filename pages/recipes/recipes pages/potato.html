Recipe.lk - Potato Curry Recipe

Ready In: 20 mins

Ingredients: 12

Serves: 3-4 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Potatoes - 4 medium, peeled and cubed
  • Onion - 1, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Green Chilies - 2, slit
  • Ginger - 1 small piece, grated
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
  • Chili Powder - 1 tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Curry Powder - 1 tbsp
  • Mustard Seeds - 1 tsp
  • Curry Leaves - 1 sprig
  • Coconut Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tbsp

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Peel and cube the potatoes. Rinse them in cold water to remove excess starch.
  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onion, garlic, ginger, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until the onions are soft and golden.
  • Add turmeric powder, chili powder, and curry powder. Stir well to combine.
  • Add the chopped tomato and cook until it softens and forms a thick paste.
  • Add the cubed potatoes and mix well, ensuring they are coated with the spice mixture.
  • Add 1 cup of water and salt to taste. Cover and cook on medium heat until the potatoes are tender (about 15-20 minutes).
  • Once the potatoes are cooked, add 1/2 cup of coconut milk and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  • Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot with rice or roti.
