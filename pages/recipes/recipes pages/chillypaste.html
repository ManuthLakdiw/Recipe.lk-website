Recipe.lk - Homemade Chili Paste

Ready In: 30 mins

Ingredients: 6

Serves: 4-6 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Fresh Red Chilies - 200g (remove seeds for less heat)
  • Garlic - 6-7 cloves
  • Ginger - 1-inch piece
  • Vinegar - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons (to preserve the paste)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Start by washing the chilies and removing the seeds if you prefer less heat.
  • In a blender or food processor, add the chilies, garlic, and ginger, then blend to a smooth paste.
  • In a pan, heat oil and add the blended paste. Stir continuously to avoid burning.
  • Once the paste is cooked and the oil has separated, add vinegar and salt, stirring to combine.
  • Allow the paste to cool completely before transferring to clean, dry bottles.
  • Seal the bottles tightly and store in a cool, dry place. This paste can be stored for several months when properly sealed.
