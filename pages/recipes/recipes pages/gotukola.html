Recipe.lk - Gotukola Sambol Recipe

Ready In: 20 mins

Ingredients: 7

Serves: 4 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Gotukola Leaves - 1 cup (finely chopped)
  • Grated Coconut - 1/2 cup
  • Green Chilies - 2 (finely chopped)
  • Onion - 1 small (finely chopped)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Lime Juice - 1 tablespoon
  • Chili Powder - 1/2 teaspoon (optional)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Finely chop the Gotukola leaves and set them aside.
  • Grate the coconut or use desiccated coconut if fresh coconut is unavailable.
  • Finely chop the green chilies and onion.
  • In a large bowl, combine the Gotukola leaves, grated coconut, green chilies, and onion.
  • Add salt and lime juice to taste. If you like it spicy, add chili powder.
  • Mix everything thoroughly until well combined.
  • Serve the Gotukola Sambol fresh with rice, roti, or other Sri Lankan dishes.
