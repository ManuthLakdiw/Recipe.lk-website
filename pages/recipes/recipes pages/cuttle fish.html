Recipe.lk - Sri Lankan Cuttlefish Curry

Ready In: 45 mins

Ingredients: 12

Serves: 4 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Cuttlefish - 500g (cleaned and cut into rings)
  • Onion - 1, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Garlic - 4 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1 inch, minced
  • Curry Leaves - a few
  • Green Chilies - 2-3, slit lengthwise
  • Chili Powder - 1 teaspoon (adjust for spice level)
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Curry Powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Coconut Milk - 1 cup
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until soft and fragrant.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, curry leaves, and green chilies. Cook until the tomatoes soften.
  • Add chili powder, turmeric powder, and curry powder. Stir well to combine the spices.
  • Add the cuttlefish rings and mix gently to coat the cuttlefish with the spices.
  • Add coconut milk and bring to a simmer. Add a little water if needed to adjust the consistency.
  • Let the curry simmer for 15-20 minutes, until the cuttlefish is tender and cooked through.
  • Adjust salt and seasoning to taste.
  • Serve the Cuttlefish Curry hot with rice or roti.
