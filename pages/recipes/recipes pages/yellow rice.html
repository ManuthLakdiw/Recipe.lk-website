Recipe.lk - Yellow Rice Recipe

Ready In: 30 mins

Ingredients: 12

Serves: 4-6 serves

INGRADIENTS

  • Rice - 2 cups (Basmati or Samba rice)
  • Coconut Milk - 2 cups
  • Water - 1 cup
  • Pandan Leaves (Rampe) - 1-2 leaves (optional)
  • Cinnamon Stick - 1 small piece
  • Cardamom Pods - 2-3
  • Cloves - 3-4
  • Turmeric Powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Onion - 1 small, finely sliced (optional)
  • Curry Leaves - a few (optional)
  • Oil or Ghee - 2 tablespoons

Optional ingradients

  • Fried Onions
  • Cashew Nuts
  • Raisins

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Gather all ingredients: rice, coconut milk, water, pandan leaves, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, turmeric, salt, onion (optional), curry leaves (optional), and oil/ghee.
  • Rinse 2 cups of rice thoroughly until the water runs clear, then drain and set aside.
  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil or ghee in a pot over medium heat.
  • Add optional sliced onion, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, cloves, and curry leaves. Saut√© until fragrant.
  • Add the rinsed rice to the pot and stir to coat it with the spices and oil.
  • In a separate pot, heat 2 cups of coconut milk until warm (do not boil).
  • Add the warm coconut milk, 1 cup of water, pandan leaves, and 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder to the rice.
  • Season with salt to taste and stir gently.
  • Cook the rice uncovered on medium heat until the liquid is mostly absorbed.
  • Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice steam for 10-15 minutes.
  • Once the rice is fully cooked, remove the lid and fluff it gently with a fork.
  • Optional: Fry cashews and raisins in a little oil until golden for garnish.
  • Remove the pandan leaves, cinnamon stick, and cardamom pods before serving.
  • Serve the Kaha Bath hot, garnished with fried onions, cashews, and raisins if desired.
