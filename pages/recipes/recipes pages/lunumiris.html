Recipe.lk - Sri Lankan Lunu Miris (Spicy Sambol)

Ready In: 10 mins

Ingredients: 5

Serves: 4 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Red Onion - 1 large, finely chopped
  • Chili Powder - 1-2 teaspoons (adjust to taste)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Lime Juice - 1 tablespoon (or more to taste)
  • Umbalakada (dried chili flakes) - 1 teaspoon (optional)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • In a bowl, combine finely chopped red onion and chili powder.
  • Add dried chili and Umbalakada (optional) for extra flavor.
  • Add salt to taste, then squeeze lime juice over the mixture and stir well.
  • Adjust the spice level by adding more chili powder or lime juice as needed.
  • Serve the Lunu Miris as a side dish with rice, bread, or roti.
