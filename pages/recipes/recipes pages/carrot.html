Recipe.lk - Carrot Sambol Recipe

Ready In: 10= mins

Ingredients: 8

Serves: 3-4 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • Carrots - 2 large, grated
  • Onion - 1 small, finely chopped
  • Green Chilies - 2, finely chopped
  • Lime Juice - 2 tbsp (or to taste)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Mustard Seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Curry Leaves - 1 sprig
  • Coconut Oil - 1 tbsp

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Peel and grate the carrots using a fine grater. Set aside in a large bowl.
  • Heat 1 tablespoon of coconut oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onion, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until the onions are soft and fragrant.
  • Remove the pan from heat and let the mixture cool slightly.
  • Add the sautéed onion mixture to the grated carrots. Mix well.
  • Add lime juice and salt to taste. Mix thoroughly until everything is well combined.
  • Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Add more lime juice or salt as desired.
  • Serve immediately as a side dish or condiment with rice, roti, or bread.
