Recipe.lk - Sri Lankan Pork Curry Recipe

Ready In: 1 hour

Ingredients: 13

Serves: 4-6 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Pork - 500g (cut into cubes)
  • Onion - 1, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1 inch, minced
  • Green Chilies - 2-3 (slit lengthwise)
  • Curry Leaves - a few
  • Cinnamon Stick - 1 small piece
  • Cloves - 2-3
  • Fennel Seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Curry Powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Chili Powder - 1 teaspoon (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Water - 1 cup

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until soft and fragrant.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, curry leaves, and green chilies. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  • Add curry powder, chili powder (optional), fennel seeds, cinnamon stick, and cloves. Stir to combine the spices.
  • Add the pork pieces and sauté for 5-7 minutes until the pork is browned and coated with the spices.
  • Pour in 1 cup of water, mix well, and bring it to a boil.
  • Lower the heat, cover, and cook for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the pork is tender and cooked through.
  • Adjust salt and seasoning to taste.
  • Serve the Sri Lankan pork curry hot with rice, roti, or bread.
