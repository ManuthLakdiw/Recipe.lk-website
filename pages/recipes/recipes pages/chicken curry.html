Recipe.lk- Sri lankan Chiken Curry Recipe

Ready In: 50 mins

Ingredients: 12

Serves: 4 serves

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken - 500g (cut into pieces)
  • Onion - 1, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1 inch, minced
  • Green Chilies - 2-3 (slit lengthwise)
  • Curry Leaves - a few
  • Cinnamon Stick - 1 small piece
  • Cardamom Pods - 2
  • Curry Powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Chili Powder - 1 teaspoon (optional, for extra heat)
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Water - 1 cup

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until soft and fragrant.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, curry leaves, and green chilies. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  • Add curry powder, chili powder (optional), turmeric powder, and salt. Stir to combine the spices.
  • Add the chicken pieces and sauté for 5-7 minutes until the chicken is browned and coated with the spices.
  • Pour in 1 cup of water, add cinnamon stick and cardamom pods, and mix well.
  • Cover the pan and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  • Adjust salt and seasoning to taste.
  • Serve the Sri Lankan chicken curry hot with rice or roti.
