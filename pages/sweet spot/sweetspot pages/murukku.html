Recipe.lk - Sini Murukku Recipe

Ready In: 45 mins

Ingredients: 8

Serves: 20-25 pieces

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice Flour - 2 cups
  • All-Purpose Flour - 1/2 cup
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Water - 1/2 cup
  • Coconut Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Cardamom Powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Sesame Seeds - 2 tbsp (optional)
  • Oil - for frying

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • In a saucepan, combine sugar and water. Heat over medium heat until the sugar dissolves completely. Let it cool.
  • In a large mixing bowl, combine rice flour, all-purpose flour, cardamom powder, and sesame seeds (if using). Mix well.
  • Gradually add the cooled sugar syrup and coconut milk to the flour mixture. Mix until it forms a smooth dough.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Using a murukku press with a star-shaped disc, press the dough into spiral shapes directly into the hot oil.
  • Fry the murukku until golden brown and crispy. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  • Let the murukku cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
  • Serve as a sweet and crunchy snack with tea or coffee.
