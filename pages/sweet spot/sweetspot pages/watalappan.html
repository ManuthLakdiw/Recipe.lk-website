Recipe.lk - Watalappan Recipe

Ready In: 1 hour

Ingredients: 6

Serves: 4-6 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup Jaggery (grated)
  • 1 cup Thick Coconut Milk
  • 5 Eggs
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Nutmeg Powder (optional)
  • Cashew Nuts (for garnish)

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • In a bowl, whisk the eggs until well combined.
  • In a saucepan, melt the jaggery with a little water until smooth.
  • Strain the jaggery syrup and mix it with coconut milk.
  • Add the whisked eggs gradually, stirring continuously.
  • Mix in the cardamom and nutmeg powder.
  • Pour the mixture into a heatproof bowl or ramekins.
  • Steam for 30-40 minutes until firm.
  • Let it cool, garnish with cashew nuts, and serve chilled.
