Recipe.lk - Vanilla Pudding Recipe

Ready In: 20 minutes + chilling time

Ingredients: 6

Serves: 4 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups Milk
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 3 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 tbsp Butter

A Step-by-Step Guide

  • In a saucepan, whisk together sugar, cornstarch, and salt.
  • Gradually add milk while stirring to avoid lumps.
  • Cook over medium heat, stirring continuously, until thickened.
  • Remove from heat and add vanilla extract and butter.
  • Pour into serving bowls and let it cool.
  • Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
  • Enjoy your creamy vanilla pudding!
